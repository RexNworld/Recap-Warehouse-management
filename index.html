Recap Rebel is focused on providing users the ability to take quick but effective snapshots of their experiences and organize them for later use. Our focus is to minimize the work of team leaders and others who have responsbilities to follow-up with customers and direct reports. Take a look at how our Recaps allow your clients to view follow-ups in a simplified format with great detail but without all the work and complexity.

Using Recap for reporting will improve your vision.


Other field execution reporting companies are highly focused on analytics, data mining etc. While in today’s world that’s important it can mean higher complexity for frontline employees and customers. Instead of just a picture captured, consumers can visibly see a point of reference on a map or blueprint and understand where your photo/video is located. In addition, CPG companies and others experience simpler views of display size/scale vs competitors.

Follow-ups take time to organize and communicate. Team leaders who work with their field teams will find that they are able to capture the moment in real time and coach employees without conventional personal note taking. At times, executional opportunities can be perceived negatively. Our Recaps allow the sender and receiver to communicate with emoji’s, gifs, photos and provide ratings to what’s captured in a fun way.

About Us


Recap Rebel was first envisioned as a superlative tool for team leaders. Our focus was to tackle the time consuming challenges of measuring field execution, simplifying follow-ups and organizing experiences captured in market.

Along our journey we found several other groups across multiple industries needed the solutions Recap Rebel had to offer. Recap Rebel is unique in how information is captured and communicated. Our partnership with Deepgram allows users to record vocal notes through audio and transcribe with HIGH precision into text. That text can then be translated into spanish or english. Note taking is tough and takes time, we simplify the work.

Rebel Technologies champions digital solutions for team leaders and reps on the frontline. We have a frontline obsession! We Rebel against convention. Our purpose is to arm our customers with the tools they need to gain share and invade competitive territory. As we love to say at Rebel Technologies, “Let the Rebellion begin!”
